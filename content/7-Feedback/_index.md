> After participating in the First Cloud AI Journey program, I have some comments and suggestions to help improve the program in future courses.

## Overall Assessment

### 1. Learning Environment Experience

I felt this was a dynamic and professional learning environment, yet still felt friendly. Activities were well-planned, and everyone was willing to exchange knowledge and support each other. This helped me feel more confident participating in workshops and completing assigned tasks.

### 2. Mentor and Organizing Committee Support

Mentors and the organizing committee closely monitored the students' learning process. Whenever I encountered technical difficulties or needed guidance, I received quick responses and very practical advice. Instead of just instructing on how to do things, they helped me understand the essence of the problem so I could solve similar situations myself.

### 3. Program Content

The program covered a wide range of topics, from foundational AWS knowledge to AI, DevOps, Security, and career guidance. The combination of theory, workshops, and practical experience from the speakers made the knowledge more accessible and helped me understand how to apply it to real-world projects.

### 4. Opportunities for Personal Development

Through the program, I not only enhanced my knowledge of cloud computing but also improved many skills such as independent research, teamwork, presentation, and problem-solving. These experiences have made me more confident in preparing for internships and future employment after graduation.

### 5. Learning Culture and Community

What I liked most was the spirit of sharing within the FCAJ community. Everyone proactively supported each other, exchanged experiences, and motivated each other to grow. The positive learning atmosphere always motivates me to continue exploring new technologies.

### 6. Organization

The workshops were well-prepared, the content was clear, and the speakers delivered the information easily. The schedule was announced in advance, allowing participants to proactively arrange their time to attend. This is a point I highly appreciate throughout the program.

## Other Questions

### What I'm Most Satisfied With

What impressed me most was the access to a lot of practical knowledge from experts working in the industry. Besides that, I also had the opportunity to expand my network with mentors and other students who share the same goals in Cloud Computing and AI.

## Areas for Improvement

I hope the program will include more practical sessions or mini-projects so that participants can apply their knowledge immediately after each topic. Additionally, there could be more Q&A sessions or sharing of professional experiences in specific specialized fields.

### Are you willing to recommend the program to others?

I am absolutely willing to recommend First Cloud AI Journey to students who are passionate about Cloud and AI. It's a great environment to learn new knowledge, gain practical experience, and build a network within the technology community.

## Suggestions and Wishes

- Organize more in-depth workshops on specific topics.

- More networking activities between students, mentors, and speakers.

- Increase the number of projects or challenges to help students hone their practical skills.

- Continue updating content related to Generative AI and new AWS services.

- If given the opportunity, I would very much like to continue participating in the program in future seasons to learn more and contribute to the FCAJ community.

For me, the First Cloud AI Journey is not just an AWS learning program, but also an opportunity to develop skills, broaden my thinking, and gain clearer career direction in the IT field. I hope the program will continue to grow and create more value for future generations of students.
